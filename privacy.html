Privacy Policy

Effective: September 28, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: If you register, we collect your email and login details.
  • Location Data: If enabled, Plot stores location data to place entries on the map.
  • Media & Notes: Any photos, notes, or media you add are stored locally or securely in the cloud if syncing is enabled.
  • Device Data: We may collect anonymized analytics (e.g., crashes, usage patterns).

2. How We Use Information

We use your data to provide app functionality, improve performance, and manage subscriptions and support requests.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Limited sharing occurs with third-party services (e.g., analytics, crash reporting, payment providers) as needed for app functionality.

4. Data Security

We use encryption and secure storage methods to protect your information, but no system is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

You can control location permissions in your device settings. You can request deletion of your account/data anytime by contacting us.

6. Children’s Privacy

Plot is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from them.

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be posted in the app.
